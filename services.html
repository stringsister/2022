Who are the StringSisters?

Heather and Naomi are twin sisters who love to travel and perform music together! Over the past decade, they have performed in some of the GTA's most beautiful concert halls and venues, including: Koerner Hall, The ROM, Graydon Hall, and Casa Loma.

Naomi - The Harpist

Naomi Jackson is a classically trained harpist who professionally performs for special events and weddings. Beginning with Celtic and Folk genres, Naomi primarily studied Classical repertoire at the Conservatory following the RCM method.

Heather - The Cellist

Heather has studied the cello for nearly a decade. The depth and warmth of the cello has always been fascinating to her. Apart from the hundreds of weddings she has played for, Heather has been a part of music recordings, traveling orchestras, and in 2019 performed with KYGO at VELD.
