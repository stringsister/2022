Elevated Live Music in Toronto

In Toronto’s competitive dining scene, atmosphere is everything. Our duo offers a unique blend of classical training and contemporary flair, making us the ideal choice for high-end establishments. We understand the nuances of dinner service—performing with a dynamic range that provides a "vibe" that guests will remember and share.

Whether you are hosting a private dinner event at a luxury venue like Graydon Hall or seeking a musical residency for your restaurant’s weekend brunch or evening service, we tailor our performance to match your brand’s aesthetic. Our repertoire includes everything from timeless Bach and Debussy to modern cinematic scores and jazz-inspired arrangements.


Why Choose The StringSisters for Your Venue?

We pride ourselves on being professional, punctual, and aesthetically aligned with luxury spaces. Our setup is compact and visually stunning, featuring the classic curves of the cello and the grandeur of the harp.

  • Conversation-Friendly: We specialize in "unplugged" or lightly amplified sets that allow guests to speak easily.
  • Diverse Repertoire: Modern Pop, Soft Jazz, and Classical masterpieces.
  • Proven Experience: Over a decade of performing in Toronto's most prestigious cultural and dining landmarks.
