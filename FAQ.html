How does the music selection work, and how many songs can we choose?

Special requests are songs currently not in our repertoire. We accept two special request songs as part of the package price.

If you are hiring us for the ceremony, we recommend you choose songs for the following:

Bridal Party entrance
Groom's entrance
Bride's entrance
Signing of the register
Bride and Groom's recessional

This is a basic template and can be customized for your ceremony needs.

How do I book The StringSisters?

We create a customized quote and contract for each event. To book our services, a $250 deposit must be sent to payments@stringsisters.ca, and all documents must be signed. After that, we're all set!

Can the harp and cello be moved from one location to another (e.g., from the ceremony to the cocktail hour)?

Yes! We can set up and perform at one location and then relocate when needed. It usually takes about 15 minutes (depending on the distance between locations) to pack, move, and re-tune our instruments.

Do musicians provide AV equipment? Do musicians need to be mic’d?

Currently, we are a completely acoustic duo and do not offer AV support. However, we plan to have a sound system for 2025 events.

We don't necessarily require microphones, but we always recommend them when possible. For ceremonies, they are not needed, but for louder settings such as cocktail hours or receptions, we recommend being mic’d.
